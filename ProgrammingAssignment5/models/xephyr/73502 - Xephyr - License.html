Blend information

  • Name: Xephyr
  • Author: LeafStorm.
  • Premalink: http://www.blendswap.com/blends/view/73502.
  • For Blender 2.52 and up.
  • Description:

     

    Finally after a year I decide to get off my butt and make som ething. This is a completely original robot concept that took me about 15 hours to make. It has a pretty simple rig and the only place I used IK is for the legs this time. I’ve also added a movable spring on its foot which can be comp ressed or stretched by scaling the bone named Spring (.R or .L). This model has over half a million vertices with sub surf and is a little slow when orbiting ar ound in the viewport but just turn Simplify on and it will be much better. Super easy. Also if there are any problems with the rig or something just tell me and I'll try to fix it. Anyway I hope you like it!
